Christ In The Storm On The Sea Of Galilee

Artist:Rembrandt van Rijn
Medium:Oil on canvas Painting
Description:A dramatic biblical scene showing Jesus calming a storm while his disciples struggle aboard a boat. The painting captures intense emotion and movement, with Rembrandt’s signature use of light and shadow. A calm sunset with orange skies and peaceful hills.

Irises In Monets Garden

Artist:Claude Monet
Medium:Oil on canvas Digital Art
Description:A peaceful garden filled with blooming irises, painted in Monet’s impressionist style. The brushstrokes are soft and colorful, evoking the beauty of nature and light.

Two Sisters Aka On The Terrace

Artist:Pierre-Auguste Renoir
Medium:Oil on canvas
Description:A charming portrait of two girls sitting on a terrace, surrounded by flowers and sunlight. Renoir’s warm tones and gentle brushwork highlight the innocence and joy of childhood.

Branches with Almond Blossom

Artist:Vincent van Gogh
Medium:Oil on canvas
Description:A delicate painting of almond tree branches in bloom against a blue sky. Van Gogh created this piece to celebrate new life and hope, using bold outlines and vibrant colors.

Creation of Adam 1510

Artist:Michelangelo Buonarroti
Medium:Fresco
Description:This iconic artwork is part of the Sistine Chapel ceiling in Vatican City. It shows God reaching out to give life to Adam, the first man, with their fingers nearly touching. The image symbolizes divine spark, human potential, and the connection between heaven and earth.

Gallery Rules

  1. Observe each painting with respect and curiosity.
  2. Do not touch or alter any artwork.
  3. Use headphones for audio guides if available.
  4. Photography may be restricted—check signage first.
  5. Maintain silence to preserve the peaceful atmosphere.

Materials Used in the Paintings

  • Oil paints (used in 4 of the 5 artworks)
  • Canvas (used for Rembrandt, Monet, Renoir, Van Gogh)
  • Fresco plaster (used for Michelangelo's Creation of Adam)
  • Brushes of various sizes
  • Natural pigments and mineral-based colors
  • Wooden frames for mounting
